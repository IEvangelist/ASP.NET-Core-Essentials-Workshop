# Conventions & Nomenclatures <i class="fas fa-tasks"></i>

There is no longer a `Global.asax` or `App_Start`. A lot of the various ways of initially configuring and launching an application have been modernized. In this chapter we'll explore the `Program` and `Startup` classes as it relates to a new common nomenclature.
